4. Short model version
It is true that some people prefer to keep everything the same in both their personal lives and society. Although there are several reasons for this preference, change should still be regarded as a positive thing because it can bring many benefits.
Admittedly, having a regular and stable life can have significant advantages. Firstly, stability often provides people with a secure income for their basic needs and daily expenses, which creates a sense of security for their families. In addition, it allows people to plan for the future more confidently. When people follow a fixed routine, they may also suffer from less anxiety because their lives are more predictable. For example, in the past in China, many university graduates were assigned stable jobs, which gave them confidence in their future.
However, stability can also have disadvantages. A fixed life path may limit people's opportunities and prevent them from developing their true potential. In some Chinese families, parents want their children to follow a life plan that has already been decided for them in order to secure a decent quality of life. Nevertheless, some young people may have talents in other fields, and forcing them to follow a fixed route can lead to a waste of their abilities. By contrast, change can be beneficial because it encourages people to learn new skills and adapt to new situations. As a result, they may have more opportunities to earn higher salaries and enjoy a better quality of life.
In conclusion, although it is understandable that some people prefer stability, change should be seen as something positive because it creates opportunities for growth and improvement.
