📝 详细解析
1. ranther → rather
正确拼写：r-a-n-t-h-e-r
记忆方法：ra-ther 分三段
例句：I would rather stay at home.
2. Forthermore → Furthermore
正确拼写：F-u-r-t-h-e-r-m-o-r-e
注意：首字母必须大写F
例句：Furthermore, the company offers good benefits.
3. posibilities → possibilities
正确拼写：p-o-s-s-i-b-i-l-i-t-i-e-s
记忆方法：双s双i（ss-ii）
例句：There are many possibilities for growth.
4. gaint → giant
正确拼写：g-i-a-n-t
记忆方法：gi不是ga
例句：It is a giant corporation.
5. motivative → motivated
正确拼写：m-o-t-i-v-a-t-e-d
注意：用-ed结尾（被动）
例句：He feels motivated to work hard.
6. respectation → respect
正确拼写：r-e-s-p-e-c-t
注意：没有-ation后缀
例句：I have great respect for my colleagues.
7. expecially → especially
正确拼写：e-s-p-e-c-i-a-l-l-y
记忆方法：es-pe-cial-ly
例句：I especially enjoy working in a team.
8. descentants → descendants
正确拼写：d-e-s-c-e-n-d-a-n-t-s
记忆方法：des-cen-dants
例句：The descendants will inherit the wealth.
9. well knowing → well-known
正确拼写：w-e-l-l-k-n-o-w-n
注意：连字符+过去分词
例句：He is a well-known expert.
10. comany → company
正确拼写：c-o-m-p-a-n-y
注意：有p（comp-a-ny）
例句：The company has grown rapidly.
11. compaies → companies
正确拼写：c-o-m-p-a-n-i-e-s
注意：直接加s（y在a后）
例句：Many companies offer good salaries.
