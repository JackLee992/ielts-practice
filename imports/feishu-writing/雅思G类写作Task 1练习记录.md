Dear Neighbour,
I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with the garbage in front of your door, which has caused inconvenience to other residents.
I have noticed that there is always some garbage around your apartment since your family moved in recently, which never happened before. I could not believe that you would put garbage in the public area.
You can imagine how unhappy I was to discover the garbage when I came back to my house, and it ruined all my good mood. I have knocked on your door several times but have got no response.
The ideal solution would be changing the custom of storing garbage in front of the door, and following the rules of community to drop them in the designated garbage area.
I hope this matter will be resolved, or I have no choice but to report it to the community management centre.
I look forward to seeing some changes.
Yours sincerely,
Jack Lee

具体错误详解
❌ 严重错误（必须改正）
1. 不可数名词
错误：some garbage in front of the door of your apartment
修正：garbage in front of your apartment
原因：garbage是不可数名词，不需要some
2. 固定搭配
错误：I have knocked your door several time
修正：I have knocked ON your door several TIMES
错误点：①缺少介词on ②time应该复数```

**3. 固定句型**
错误：no choose but to report
修正：no CHOICE but to report
错误点：choose这里错误，应该是choice

**4. 动名词搭配**
错误：look forward to hearing/see
修正：look forward to SEEING
原因：look forward to后面必须用动名词```
⚠️ 中式表达问题
1. 过于口语化
原文：I could not believe that putting garbage in public area is the habit of yours!
问题：表达太口语化，缺乏正式性
修正：I was surprised to discover this practice
2. 翻译腔重
原文：it ruined all my delighted emotion
问题：这种表达在英文中很不自然
修正：it completely ruined my good mood
时态问题
时态搭配混乱：
错误：since your family has moved in recently
修正：since your family MOVED in
原因：since后面的时间点用过去时，表示状态的持续
