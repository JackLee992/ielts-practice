🎯 学习目标
掌握a/an/the的区别
掌握5个高频介词搭配
📝 早晨练习题（改错10题）
请找出每句的错误并改正：
I would like to have a advice.
改正：去粗a（advice是不可数名词）
They live in a United States.
改正：the United States（国名前用the）
Please knock in the door.
改正：knock on the door
The company has an impact for the environment.
改正：impact on the environment
I look forward to see you.
改正：look forward to seeing you
There is a university nearby.
改正：a university（university以辅音音素开头）
He works with Google.
改正：works for Google（在某公司工作）
The meeting was held in a Monday.
改正：on Monday
She is a European.
改正：a European（European以/j/开头）
I have no__选择__but to__go__.
改正：no choice but to go

答案：
去掉a
a → the
in → on
for → on
see → seeing
正确
with → for
in → on
正确
选择 → choice

📚 晚间复习
背诵被动语态公式：

一般过去时：was/were + past participle
进行时：am/is/are being + past participle
完成时：have/has been + past participle
