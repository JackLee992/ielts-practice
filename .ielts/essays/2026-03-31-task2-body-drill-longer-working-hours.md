## 题目

In many countries, people are working longer hours than before.

Why is this happening?

Is this a positive or negative development?

## Body Assignment

- Body 1 job: explain why people are working longer hours
- Body 2 job: explain why this is a negative development

## 中文四行计划

### Body 1

1. 中心句：现在很多人工作时间更长，主要是因为生活成本上升和职场竞争加剧
2. 解释：房租、教育和日常开支都更贵，很多人只能接受加班或额外兼职
3. 结果：长工时被当成保住收入和争取晋升的代价
4. 例子：大城市上班族常常为了完成业绩目标而晚下班

### Body 2

1. 中心句：我认为这是负面发展，因为长时间工作会损害健康和家庭生活
2. 解释：工作占据大部分时间后，人更容易压力大、睡眠少，也没有精力休息或锻炼
3. 结果：生活质量下降，长期来看工作效率也可能变差
4. 例子：经常加班的父母容易错过家庭陪伴时间，和孩子关系也会变弱

## Guided English Body Paragraphs

### Body 1

Many people are working longer hours because living costs have risen and competition in the job market has become much stronger. In order to pay for housing, education, and daily expenses, many employees feel that they must accept overtime or even take on extra work. As a result, long working hours are often seen as the price of financial security and career progress. For example, office workers in large cities frequently stay late to meet targets and protect their income.

### Body 2

I believe this is largely a negative development because excessive working hours can damage both personal well-being and family life. When people spend most of their time at work, they are more likely to feel stressed, sleep less, and have little time for rest or exercise. This can reduce their quality of life and may even lower productivity in the long run. For instance, parents who regularly work late often miss family meals and find it difficult to maintain close relationships with their children.
