## Errors Found

| Draft | Correction | Type |
|---|---|---|
| `dishwaher` | `dishwasher` | spelling |
| `one of retailers` | `one of your retailers` | missing possessive |
| `only have worked` | `only worked` | tense |
| `at last sunday` | `last Sunday` | no preposition before last Sunday |
| `there is no water run out` | `no water came out` | sentence pattern |
| `totally can not` | `was completely unable to function` | word order + formality |
| `the shop I bought from` | `the shop where I purchased it` | formality |
| `short of hands` | `short-staffed` | collocation |
| `recent days` | delete or `at the moment` | expression |
| `change a functional new one` | `replace it with a new one` | collocation |
| `within one week` | `less than a week ago` | expression |

## Rules Saved

1. `last Sunday / last week` ‚Äî no preposition before these time expressions
2. `replace it with a new one` ‚Äî not `change a new one`
3. `short-staffed` ‚Äî formal way to say lacking staff
4. Use `purchased` not `bought` in formal complaint letters

## Coach-Corrected Full Letter

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to complain about a dishwasher I purchased from one of your retailers two weeks ago. I would be grateful if you could look into this matter.

Unfortunately, the dishwasher stopped working after only one day of use. When I tried to use it after dinner last Sunday, no water came out and the machine was completely unable to function.

I contacted the shop where I purchased the dishwasher, but they were completely unhelpful. They informed me that they were short-staffed and unable to deal with my complaint at the moment.

As I purchased the dishwasher less than a week ago, I would appreciate it if you could replace it with a fully functioning new one at your earliest convenience.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
JackLee
