# Task 1 Request Letter — Lost Belongings at Hotel

## Date

2026-03-30

## Prompt

You have just returned from a trip abroad and realised you left some belongings in the hotel room. Write a letter to the hotel manager. In your letter:
- describe what you left behind
- explain where in the room you think the items are
- ask the hotel to send the items to you

## Your Draft

Dear Sir/Madom,

I am writing to inform you that I accidentally left my powerbank in my room during my recent stay from Feb,2nd to Feb 5th. The powerbank is a small white one with Xiaomi brand logo on it. I remember I left it on the bed, maybe under one pillow. I would be grateful if you could arragnge to send the item to my address: Tiantan Road, 26, Beijing. I am happy to cover any postage costs.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Your faithfully,
JackLee

## Errors Found

| Draft | Correction | Type |
|---|---|---|
| `Dear Sir/Madom` | `Dear Sir/Madam` | spelling |
| `Feb,2nd` | `2 February` or `February 2nd` | date format |
| `arragnge` | `arrange` | spelling |
| `Your faithfully` | `Yours faithfully` | fixed expression |
| only one item, ~100 words | add second item, reach 150+ words | content insufficient |

## Rules Saved

1. `Yours faithfully` — must have the s, never `Your faithfully`
2. Date format: `2 February` or `February 2nd` — no comma after month abbreviation
3. Word count strategy: add a second item + add a confirmation email request sentence

## Coach-Corrected Full Letter

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to inform you that I accidentally left some belongings in my room during my recent stay from 2 February to 5 February. I would be very grateful if you could help me retrieve them.

I believe I left two items behind: a white Xiaomi power bank and a black charging cable. The power bank is small with the Xiaomi logo clearly visible on it.

I think the power bank is on the bed, possibly under one of the pillows. The charging cable may be near the desk, as I recall using it there on my last evening.

I would be grateful if you could arrange to send both items to my address: 26 Tiantan Road, Beijing. I am happy to cover any postage costs, and would appreciate a confirmation email once the items have been located.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours faithfully,
JackLee
