# Task 1 Request Letter — Android Development Course — 2026-04-02

## Prompt

You saw an advertisement for an Android development course for everyone. Write a letter to the course provider asking for more information. In your letter:
- explain why you are interested in the course
- ask about the course structure and schedule
- ask about the tuition fee and any possible discounts

## User's Final Draft

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to ask for more information about the course "Android Development for Everyone".

I am interested in this course because I have long wanted to become a professional engineer, but I have not had the chance to do so. However, the advertisement for your course has encouraged me to pursue my goal.

Could you please tell me more about the structure and detailed schedule of the course? I would also like to know the total tuition fee. In addition, could you please tell me whether any discounts are available for college students?

I look forward to your reply at your earliest convenience.

Yours faithfully,
Jack Lee

## Rules Saved

1. Use `the course "X"` or `"X" course` for named courses.
2. Use `look forward to`, not `look forward for`.
3. In formal letters, `Dear Sir or Madam` and `Yours faithfully` should be exact.
4. For Task 1 request letters, keep each bullet to one clean sentence.
