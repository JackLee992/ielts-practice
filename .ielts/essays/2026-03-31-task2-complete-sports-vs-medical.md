# Task 2 完整 Essay — 2026-03-31

## 题目

Some people think that governments should spend money on building public sports facilities for everyone. Others think that money should be spent on improving medical services. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

## 完整改写版（Cleaned Essay）

People hold different views about whether governments should prioritise investing in public sports facilities or improving medical services. While both arguments have merit, I believe that improving healthcare is more important, as it can significantly reduce mortality rates.

Investing in public sports facilities can help residents develop regular exercise habits. These facilities are normally free or available at a low cost, which allows citizens on lower incomes to exercise consistently. For example, community fitness centres and public swimming pools enable more people to participate in physical activity. Therefore, the construction of such facilities can fundamentally improve the overall health of society.

On the other hand, improving medical services can save lives more directly. Enhanced medical resources allow more people to receive timely treatment, which can significantly reduce mortality rates. For instance, during a pandemic, governments that built more hospitals and trained additional medical staff were able to respond to public health crises far more effectively. As a result, investment in healthcare tends to produce more immediate and far-reaching benefits for society.

In conclusion, while building public sports facilities can encourage healthier lifestyles, I believe that investing in medical services is the more important priority, as it produces more immediate and far-reaching benefits. Governments should therefore allocate a greater proportion of public funding to healthcare.
