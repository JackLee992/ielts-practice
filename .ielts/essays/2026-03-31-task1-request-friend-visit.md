# Task 1 — Request Letter (Friend Visit) — 2026-03-31 Evening

## Prompt

You are going to visit a foreign city for the first time. You want to stay with your English-speaking friend who lives there.

Write a letter to your friend. In your letter:
- explain why you are visiting the city
- say when you plan to arrive and how long you will stay
- ask for some help or information about the city

---

## User's Original Draft

> Dear friend, it has been a while since when meet last time. How have you been recently? I am writing to inform you that I am planning to visit your city next week. I'd like to have a trip with your city since last time we talked about the famous historical places. And because I am during my vocation, so I will have a whole week for the visiting. The exact time of my arrival will be 8:00AM next monday at the west airport. Furthermore, I would be appreciate if you could give me some details about which places are famous and worth to visit. Don't forget to pick me up! Hope to see you soon! Your sincierly, JackLee.

**Word count: ~130 (below 150 minimum)**

---

## Error Log

### Spelling

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| sincierly | sincerely |
| vocation | vacation |

### Grammar & Collocation

| Original | Corrected | Rule |
|----------|-----------|------|
| since when meet last time | since we last met | 时态 + 语序 |
| have a trip with your city | visit your city | 搭配错误 |
| I am during my vocation | I am on holiday / on vacation | during 不用于此；vocation = 职业 |
| because...so | because / so (不能连用) | 二选一 |
| the visiting | the trip | 冗余 |
| I would be appreciate | I would appreciate it if | 结构错误 |
| worth to visit | worth visiting | worth + V-ing，无 to |
| Your sincierly | Yours sincerely | 固定结尾 |

---

## Key Lessons

1. 字数不足的根本原因：每个要点只写一句就停——每段至少 3 句（要点 → 细节 → 补充）
2. 写给朋友的信语气可以随意，编一个名字比留空格更自然
3. `worth visiting`，不是 `worth to visit`
4. `on holiday / on vacation`，不是 `during my vocation`

## Corrected Version

Dear Sarah,

It's been ages since we last met — hope you're doing well! I'm writing because I'm planning to visit your city next week and I'd love to catch up.

We talked about those famous historical spots last time, and I've been wanting to check them out ever since. I'm on holiday at the moment, so I've got a whole week free. I'm arriving at the West Airport at 8:00 AM next Monday.

I would love some tips on which places are worth visiting. I am particularly interested in historical sites and local markets. Any restaurant recommendations would be a bonus! Oh, and any chance you could pick me up from the airport?

Can't wait to see you!

Best,
JackLee
