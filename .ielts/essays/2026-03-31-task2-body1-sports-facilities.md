# Task 2 Body 1 练习 — 2026-03-31

## 题目

Some people think that governments should spend money on building public sports facilities for everyone. Others think that money should be spent on improving medical services. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

## 中文四行计划（Body 1）

1. 中心句：政府投资公共运动设施能帮助普通市民养成锻炼习惯
2. 解释：免费或低价开放，让经济条件一般的人也能定期运动
3. 例子：社区健身房和公共游泳池使更多人参与体育活动
4. 收口：运动设施建设从根本上有助于提升整体公众健康水平

## 原始输出

Investment making on public sport facilities can help residents cultivate a good habit of excerice. These devices normally free or at a low cost for public, which makes citizens with low incomes can also enjoy fixed workout activities. For example, residential excercise center and public swimming pools let more people to participate into excercise. Thus, the instruction of public fitness devices can significantly boost the health level of whole society.

## 主要错误

| 原文 | 修正 | 错误类型 |
|---|---|---|
| Investment making on | Investing in | 词组错误 |
| excerice / excercise | exercise | 拼写（出现2次）|
| devices | facilities | 词汇选择 |
| normally free（缺动词）| are normally free | 语法 |
| makes...can | allows...to | 句式混用 |
| fixed workout | regular exercise | 词汇不自然 |
| residential excercise center | community fitness centres | 词汇错误 |
| participate into | participate in | 介词错误 |
| let more people to | allow more people to | let 后不加 to |
| instruction | construction | 词汇错误 |
| the health level of whole society | the overall health of society | 表达不自然 |

## 改写版

Investing in public sports facilities can help residents develop regular exercise habits. These facilities are normally free or available at a low cost, which allows citizens on lower incomes to exercise consistently. For example, community fitness centres and public swimming pools enable more people to participate in physical activity. Therefore, the construction of such facilities can fundamentally improve the overall health of society.

## 估分：Band 5.0–5.5

## 今日规则

1. facilities vs devices：运动场所 = sports facilities，devices 是电子设备
2. allow sb to do / let sb do / make sb do — 三个不能混用
3. exercise 拼写：不是 excerice / excercise

# Task 2 Body 2 练习 — 2026-03-31

## 中文四行计划（Body 2）

1. 中心句：改善医疗服务能更直接地挽救生命
2. 解释：医疗资源提升让更多人获得及时治疗，降低死亡率
3. 例子：增加医院床位和培训医护人员，有效应对公共健康危机
4. 收口：医疗投入回报更即时，影响更深远

## 原始输出

On the other hand, better medical services can directly save more lives. The improvement of medical resources can helps more poeple get instant treatment, which can significantly reduce the public mortility. For instance, during pandamic, Chinese government built more hospitals and increased the staff of doctors, which makes it more efficient to deal with public health crisis. As a result, medital investment can have more direct and long-term inpacts.

## 主要错误

| 原文 | 修正 | 错误类型 |
|---|---|---|
| can helps | can help | 情态动词后原形 |
| poeple | people | 拼写 |
| public mortility | mortality | 拼写 + 用词 |
| instant treatment | timely treatment | 词汇不自然 |
| during pandamic | during a pandemic | 拼写 + 缺冠词 |
| the staff of doctors | the number of medical staff | 词汇错误 |
| makes it more efficient | enabling them to respond more effectively | 句式不自然 |
| medital | medical | 拼写 |
| inpacts | impacts | 拼写 |
| more direct and long-term | more immediate and far-reaching | 搭配不自然 |

## 改写版

On the other hand, improving medical services can save lives more directly. Enhanced medical resources allow more people to receive timely treatment, which can significantly reduce mortality rates. For instance, during a pandemic, governments that built more hospitals and trained additional medical staff were able to respond to public health crises far more effectively. As a result, investment in healthcare tends to produce more immediate and far-reaching benefits for society.

## 估分：Band 5.0–5.5

## 今日规则（Body 2）

1. 情态动词后原形：can help ✅ / can helps ❌
2. 拼写清单：people / mortality / pandemic / medical / impacts
3. 可数名词单数前必须有冠词：during a pandemic ✅ / during pandemic ❌
