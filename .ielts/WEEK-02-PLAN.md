2. Speaking 10-15 min
3. Listening 15-20 min

## Daily Check-In Format

Each evening, report in 4 lines:

1. `Planned:` what you intended to do
2. `Done:` what you actually finished
3. `Blocked:` what broke or slowed you down
4. `Saved rule:` one rule or repeated error from today

This is the format we will use for daily tracking.
