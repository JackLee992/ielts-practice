## Daily Plan

### Monday 2026-03-30
**Morning (10-15 min)**: 回看听力 RULES.md 最新 10 条规则，默念 3 遍：
- First mention is not the answer.
- Exact word, not topic gist.
- No blank in Part 1 or Section 4.

**Evening (45-60 min)**:
- Listening 30 min: Part 4 note completion 1 组，盯词性预测、精确名词、句尾答案
- Writing 20 min: Task 2 审题分盒子 + 两段中文四句
- Speaking 10 min: 1 个 podcast 片段，3-sentence retell + personal response

**Minimum**: Part 4 小练 1 组 + 口语 1 条录音

---

### Tuesday 2026-03-31 ← TODAY
**Morning (10 min)**: 看昨天听力错题，回答两句：trap 是什么 / final answer signal 是什么

**Evening (50-60 min)**:
- Listening 30 min: Part 3 multiple choice 1 组，标出 but/actually/however/in fact/so
- Writing 20-25 min: 把周一最稳的中文四句翻成英文 body（不跑题、不断链）
- Speaking 10 min: Part 1 话题 4-5 题，每题 20-30 秒

**Minimum**: 至少完成 1 个英文 body

### Wednesday 2026-04-01
**Morning (10 min)**: 听写 10 个高频错词：
camera / hospital / competition / flooding / agriculture / maps / taxation / destruction / dentist / weekend

**Evening (45-55 min)**:
- Listening 20-25 min: Section 1 spelling & format 小练，重点数字、时间、单复数

### Thursday 2026-04-02

- Two answers means two confirmations.
- Mentioned is not recommended.

**Evening (50-65 min)**:
- Listening 30-35 min: Part 3 matching 或 choose-two 1 组，收齐两个答案

- Speaking 10 min: Part 2，背景-事件-转折-感受，说 2 分钟

**Minimum**: Part 3 小练 1 组 + Part 2 录音 1 条

- 听力 full mock 是否达到或接近 27/40
