# Writing Session — 2026-04-01

## Topic
Some people think that robots are very important for human's future development. Others, however, think that robots are a dangerous invention that could have negative effects on society. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

## Chinese Plan
- Body 1: 机器人提供便利，提升生活质量。
- Body 2: 机器人导致失业，收入下降。

## User's Original Output (Body 2)
However, robots sometimes are treated as dangerous as it may lead to high unemployment rate. There are more and more robots getting hired instead of using human, which can definitelly cause the original positions people loose their jobs. Further more, people who loose their jobs may not switch to a new occupation smoothly and their salaries will drop deeply. For example, resturants and hotels are replacing human workers with food dilivery roboots.

## Detailed Correction
- high unemployment rate -> **a** high unemployment rate
- definitelly -> **definitely**
- resturants -> **restaurants**
- dilivery -> **delivery**
- roboots -> **robots**
- loose -> **lose**
- Further more -> **Furthermore**

## High-impact Expressions to Master
- `perceived as dangerous` (instead of treated as dangerous)
- `result in a surge in unemployment` (instead of lead to high unemployment)
- `suffer a significant loss of income` (instead of salaries drop deeply)
- `adapt to new roles` (instead of switch to new occupation)
- `replace human staff with robots` (instead of replace human workers)
