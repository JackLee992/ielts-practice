## 1. Logic Chain Drills (Chinese to English)

### Q1: Why some people prefer stability?
- **Logic Chain**: Stable life rhythm -> predictable income and expenditure -> lower financial risk -> peace of mind / better future planning.
- **Key Sentence**: A stable life generally provides people with a stable income. This predictability can significantly reduce anxiety.

### Q2: Why change is positive?
- **Logic Chain**: Exposure to new environments/education -> gaining more skills -> bringing more opportunities and possibilities -> personal growth and life improvement.
- **Key Sentence**: Embracing change enables people to unlock their potential. Thus, change should be perceived as positive as it creates opportunities for growth.
