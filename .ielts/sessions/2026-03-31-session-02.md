## Date

## Today's Bottleneck

The main task is to transfer the morning rules to a new prompt without drifting back into translation-heavy English.

## Today's Objective

Write two complete English body paragraphs on a new double-question Task 2 prompt.

Today is still a body-control day, not a full-essay day.

## Target Skill

`point -> reason -> result -> example`

## Prompt

In many countries, people are working longer hours than before.

Why is this happening?

Is this a positive or negative development?

## Body Assignment

- Body 1 job: explain why people are working longer hours
- Body 2 job: explain why this is a negative development

## Pre-Writing Rule

Do not write English first.

For each body paragraph, write these four short Chinese slots:

1. Center
2. Why
3. Result
4. Example

## Carry-Over Rules

1. Each body paragraph has one job only.
2. Use a simpler correct sentence instead of a longer broken one.
3. After modal verbs, always use the base form.
4. Singular countable nouns need an article.
5. Use stable collocations such as `living costs`, `financial security`, and `family life`.

## Your Required Output

### Step 1: Chinese four-line plan

Write:

- Body 1 Chinese four lines
- Body 2 Chinese four lines

### Step 2: English body paragraphs

Turn each Chinese four-line plan into one English paragraph.

Limits:

- 4 sentences per paragraph
- about 70-90 words per paragraph
- simple correct English is better than ambitious broken English

## Self-Check Before You Stop

Ask:

1. Does Body 1 only answer why people work longer hours?
2. Does Body 2 only judge whether this trend is positive or negative?
3. Does each paragraph keep one center only?
4. Did I keep the order point -> reason -> result -> example?
5. Did I avoid risky wording that I cannot control?

## If You Get Stuck

Use one of these safe sentence frames:

- Many people are working longer hours because ...
- This is mainly because ...
- As a result, ...
- For example, ...
- I believe this is a negative development because ...
