# IELTS Phrase Bank

Use this file for natural expressions worth reusing in Writing Task 2.

Do not memorize them blindly.

Instead:

1. pick 2-3 phrases before writing
2. fit them into one clear paragraph center
3. keep the sentence simple if grammar starts to break

## Work From Home / Flexibility

- `remote work`
- `work from home`
- `save commuting time`
- `travel to and from work`
- `give employees greater flexibility`
- `give employees more control over their daily schedule`
- `handle family responsibilities`
- `deal with family duties`
- `maintain a better work-life balance`
- `use that time for personal tasks`

## Office Work / Teamwork

- `office-based work`
- `work in an office`
- `make communication more direct`
- `make coordination more efficient`
- `improve teamwork`
- `face-to-face communication`
- `face-to-face interaction`
- `schedule meetings more easily`
- `solve problems more quickly`
- `quicker responses`
- `closer coordination among coworkers`

## Natural Result Shells

- `..., giving employees ...`
- `..., allowing them to ...`
- `..., which helps ...`
- `with ..., ...`
- `..., leading to ...`

## Safe Model Lines

- `Working from home saves commuting time, giving employees more freedom to handle family responsibilities.`
- `Remote work gives people more flexibility, allowing them to maintain a better work-life balance.`
- `Working in an office improves teamwork, with quicker responses and closer coordination among coworkers.`
- `Office-based work makes communication more direct, with face-to-face interaction helping staff solve problems more quickly.`

## Replace These Weak Or Translated Phrases

- `home responsibilities` -> `family responsibilities`
- `take the balance with work and life` -> `maintain a better work-life balance`
- `closed coordination` -> `closer coordination`
- `face to face communication` -> `face-to-face communication`
- `better working result` -> `work more efficiently` / `be more effective`
- `a chill environment` -> `a more relaxed working environment`

## Review Rule

For each new Task 2 on a work or lifestyle topic:

1. choose one paragraph center
2. choose one natural result shell
3. choose 2-3 phrases from this bank
4. do not add a second paragraph center just to use more phrases

## Ongoing Accumulation Workflow

When a non-natural or translated expression appears in practice:

1. replace it with one natural version
2. add the weak expression and its replacement to this file when it is reusable
3. reuse the corrected expression in the next drill or next full essay
4. if the same weak pattern repeats, turn it into a saved rule in `.ielts/RULES.md`
